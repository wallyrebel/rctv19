# Flexi Parts Articulated Joins 2026 Burnside Music Fest Vendor Lineup

The Burnside Music Fest is thrilled to announce the addition of Flexi Parts Articulated to its 2026 vendor lineup, bringing a fresh and creative shopping experience to the annual celebration of Hill Country blues and live music.

## What to Expect

Flexi Parts Articulated specializes in innovative 3D-printed creations that appeal to both children and adults. Their colorful collection includes:

- Flexible articulated animals
- Imaginative dragon designs
- Unique character figures
- Other playful 3D-printed pieces

These handcrafted items serve as excellent gifts and collectibles, offering festival-goers a chance to take home one-of-a-kind souvenirs that showcase modern manufacturing creativity.

## Festival Details

**Date:** Saturday, June 6, 2026  
**Location:** Walnut Street, Historic Downtown Ripley, Mississippi

## Plan Your Visit

While enjoying the festival's live music performances, local food vendors, and shopping opportunities, be sure to make a stop at the Flexi Parts Articulated booth. The vendor spotlight is part of the broader effort to support local artisans and businesses while celebrating Ripley's vibrant cultural scene.

Whether you're a collector of unique items, searching for the perfect gift, or simply curious about 3D printing innovations, Flexi Parts Articulated promises an engaging experience at one of the region's premier music festivals.
