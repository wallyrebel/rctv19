# CB&S Bank Backs 2026 Burnside Music Fest with Sponsorship

CB&S Bank has been recognized as a key sponsor of the 2026 Burnside Music Fest, demonstrating the financial institution's ongoing dedication to supporting cultural events and community initiatives in the Ripley area.

## Supporting Community and Culture

The sponsorship reflects CB&S Bank's commitment to fostering vibrant cultural experiences and revitalizing the historic downtown Ripley district. By backing the Burnside Music Fest, the bank is helping to ensure that the community can continue to enjoy quality live music programming while supporting local economic development.

## Festival Impact

The Burnside Music Fest has become an important annual event in the Hill Country blues scene, drawing music enthusiasts and supporting local artists. Corporate partnerships like CB&S Bank's sponsorship are essential to making such community events financially viable and successful.

## Local Investment

The bank's investment in the festival underscores the importance of local business involvement in community cultural initiatives. Such sponsorships help strengthen the connection between financial institutions and the communities they serve, while simultaneously enhancing the cultural landscape of downtown Ripley.

The 2026 Burnside Music Fest is shaping up to be another memorable celebration of live music and community spirit, with supporters like CB&S Bank making it all possible.
