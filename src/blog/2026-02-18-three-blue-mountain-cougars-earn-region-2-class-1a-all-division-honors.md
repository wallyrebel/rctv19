**BLUE MOUNTAIN, Miss.** — The Blue Mountain Cougars are celebrating a major postseason accomplishment as three standout players have been named to the **Region 2 Class 1A All-Division Team**.

Congratulations go out to:

- **Chance Venson** – All Division Team
- **Jordan Hall** – All Division Team
- **Trey Houston** – All Division Team

The trio played a pivotal role in leading the Cougars throughout the season, showcasing leadership, consistency, and impact performances on both ends of the floor.

Venson’s steady play and scoring ability made him a constant threat, while Hall’s presence in the paint and ability to control the tempo gave Blue Mountain a strong interior anchor. Houston’s athleticism and defensive intensity helped fuel the Cougars in key region matchups.

Earning All-Division recognition in Region 2 Class 1A is no small feat. The honor reflects not only individual talent but also dedication, work ethic, and the respect of coaches across the region.

Head coach **Britt Lindley** praised the group for their leadership and character throughout the season.

> “Going to miss these three guys next year! Couldn’t ask for a better group of guys to lead our team.”

The Cougars’ success this season was built on teamwork, discipline, and a commitment to improvement — and having three players recognized at the all-division level speaks volumes about the strength of the program.

With Venson, Hall, and Houston leaving a strong legacy, Blue Mountain basketball looks to build on the foundation they helped establish.

Congratulations to these outstanding student-athletes on a well-earned honor.
