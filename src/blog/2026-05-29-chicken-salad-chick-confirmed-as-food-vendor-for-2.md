# Chicken Salad Chick Brings Culinary Flavor to 2026 Burnside Music Fest

The organizers of the 2026 Burnside Music Fest have announced an exciting addition to their vendor lineup: Chicken Salad Chick, a popular food establishment known for its signature chicken salad offerings and fresh sandwiches.

## A Perfect Pairing: Food and Music

Attendees of the upcoming festival will have the opportunity to enjoy Chicken Salad Chick's delicious menu while soaking in a full day of live Hill Country Blues performances in the heart of Historic Downtown Ripley, Mississippi. The festival promises to be a comprehensive entertainment experience, combining quality food with authentic musical performances.

## What to Expect

Chicken Salad Chick will be operating a food vendor booth at the festival, serving their specialty items including chicken salad, sandwiches, and other menu favorites. This addition to the vendor roster provides festival attendees with convenient dining options while they enjoy the live music and community atmosphere.

## Festival Details

The 2026 Burnside Music Fest is shaping up to be a major event for the Ripley community, featuring Hill Country Blues acts and a growing roster of food and craft vendors. Organizers continue to build excitement for the festival by announcing new vendor partnerships and entertainment lineups.

Festival-goers are encouraged to plan their visit to Historic Downtown Ripley and make sure to stop by the Chicken Salad Chick booth to sample their offerings during the festivities.
