# BMCU Softball Team Honored with SSAC Champions of Character Award

Blue Mountain Christian University's softball program has earned significant recognition beyond the playing field, receiving the SSAC Mayor Todd Strange Female Champions of Character Team Award.

## Recognition of Excellence

The honor highlights the team's exceptional commitment to integrity, sportsmanship, and community service. The award, named after Mayor Todd Strange, represents the Southern States Athletic Conference's dedication to recognizing student-athletes who exemplify character and leadership both on and off the diamond.

## Historic Season Achievement

The recognition comes during what has been described as a historic season for the BMCU softball program. The team's achievement of the Champions of Character award underscores that success extends far beyond winning games—it encompasses the values and principles that define the student-athletes as individuals and representatives of their institution.

## Community Impact

The award reflects the team's dedication to making a positive impact within their community and university. By receiving this honor, BMCU's softball program demonstrates that athletic excellence and personal character development go hand in hand, setting an example for other programs across the conference.

This recognition serves as a testament to the coaching staff's emphasis on developing well-rounded student-athletes who understand the importance of integrity, leadership, and service to others.
