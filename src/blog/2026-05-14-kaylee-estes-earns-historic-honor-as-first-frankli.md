# Kaylee Estes Named First Franklin Financial Tippah County Sports Female Athlete of the Year

Kaylee Estes has earned the distinction of being named the 2025-2026 Franklin Financial Tippah County Sports Female Athlete of the Year, making her the inaugural recipient of this prestigious award.

The honor recognizes Estes' outstanding athletic performance and dedication to sports in Tippah County. As the first athlete to receive this award, Estes sets the standard for future recipients and represents the excellence that Franklin Financial is committed to celebrating within the local sports community.

This recognition underscores Estes' contributions to her athletic pursuits and her impact on Tippah County's sports landscape. The award, sponsored by Franklin Financial, highlights the organization's commitment to supporting and recognizing exceptional athletes in the region.

Estes' selection as the inaugural award winner reflects her exceptional talent, work ethic, and positive influence on her teams and community. The award serves as a testament to her athletic abilities and the bright future ahead for this accomplished athlete.
