# The Cut-Off Seeks Food Trucks for Summer Opportunity as Chula's Takes Break

The Cut-Off, a popular gathering spot in Ripley, Mississippi, is launching a search for food truck vendors to fill a temporary gap in its food service offerings.

## The Opportunity

With Chula's, the venue's regular food partner, taking a well-deserved break from July 4-20, The Cut-Off is looking for food trucks to maintain its reputation for quality dining and hospitality. This presents a unique opportunity for food vendors seeking to expand their presence and introduce their businesses to the Ripley community.

## Flexible Arrangements

The venue is welcoming food trucks for varying commitments—whether vendors can commit to a single day or multiple days throughout the closure period, The Cut-Off is eager to work with them. This flexibility makes the opportunity accessible to food trucks with different operational schedules.

## How to Get Involved

Interested food truck operators can reach out directly to discuss availability and logistics:

- **Phone:** 662-512-0226
- **Text:** 662-815-0021

**Location:** The Cut-Off, 204 South Commerce Street, Ripley, MS

## Community Impact

By stepping in during this period, food trucks will help The Cut-Off maintain its role as a community hub for good food and social gathering. The venue's message emphasizes that this collaboration will keep "the good food and good vibes going" until Chula's returns from their summer break, highlighting the importance of continued service to the local community.
