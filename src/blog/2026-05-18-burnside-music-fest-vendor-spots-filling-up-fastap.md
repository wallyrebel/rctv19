# Burnside Music Fest Vendor Opportunities Available—Don't Miss Out

The countdown is on for Burnside Music Fest, and organizers are making a final call for vendors to join what promises to be an unforgettable celebration of music, art, and community in Downtown Ripley.

## Limited Spots Available for Local Makers and Businesses

With the festival just weeks away, a handful of arts and crafts vendor spots remain available for local makers, artists, and small business owners looking to showcase their work. The event is shaping up to be a major draw, combining live music performances, local food vendors, and artisan crafts in a vibrant community gathering.

## Time to Act Is Now

Organizers emphasize that interested vendors should apply soon, as available spots are limited and expected to fill quickly. This is a prime opportunity for local entrepreneurs and creative professionals to gain exposure and connect with community members who value supporting homegrown talent.

## How to Get Involved

Local makers and small business owners interested in becoming part of the festival can sign up through the vendor registration portal. The application process is straightforward, allowing businesses to secure their spot and begin planning their festival presence.

The Burnside Music Fest represents more than just a day of entertainment—it's a celebration of local culture and community spirit. For those looking to support local businesses and makers, or for vendors seeking to expand their reach, this festival offers the perfect platform.

**Interested in becoming a vendor? Applications are being accepted now, so don't delay—secure your spot today.**
