# Blue Mountain Christian Golf Duo Achieves All-American Honors

Two standout golfers from Blue Mountain Christian University have earned prestigious NAIA Second-Team All-American honors, capping off successful sophomore campaigns on the course.

Cobey Riddle and Alex Nordstrom, both key contributors to the No. 12-ranked Eagles men's golf program, received the recognition after demonstrating exceptional performance throughout the season. The honor reflects their individual excellence and their significant impact on the team's overall success.

## Season Highlights

Both players posted top-25 individual finishes during the season, showcasing their competitive prowess at the collegiate level. Their consistent play proved instrumental in helping Blue Mountain Christian maintain its position among the nation's elite golf programs.

## Team Success

The recognition of Riddle and Nordstrom underscores the strength of the Eagles' golf program. With these two sophomores performing at an All-American level, Blue Mountain Christian's men's golf team has established itself as a national contender, achieving a top-15 national ranking this season.

The All-American selections provide a strong foundation for the program's future, with both players still having two more years of eligibility remaining. Their early success at the collegiate level suggests continued excellence ahead for the Eagles golf program.
