# Summer Celebration Comes to Downtown Ripley Tomorrow Night

Residents and visitors looking for the perfect Friday evening have plenty of reasons to head downtown to Ripley tomorrow. The city is rolling out a comprehensive lineup of entertainment and activities designed to celebrate summer and bring the community together.

## Evening Schedule and Activities

The festivities begin with extended shopping hours, with downtown merchants staying open until 7:00 PM. As the sun begins to set, the entertainment ramps up with free live music featuring performer Trevor Wilson starting at 7:00 PM at The Cut-Off.

Food options will be readily available throughout the evening, with Chula's Food Truck offering dinner selections. For those looking to cool off during the summer warmth, The Gathering Porch will be serving drinks.

## Perfect Setup for a Relaxing Evening

Organizers are encouraging attendees to bring lawn chairs and settle in for a relaxing summer evening with friends and family. The casual atmosphere aims to create an ideal community gathering spot downtown.

## Fireworks Grand Finale

The evening will culminate with the City of Ripley's Fireworks Show at 9:30 PM, which will be launched near Ripley High School. Attendees have flexibility in choosing their viewing location—whether they prefer the official viewing area or want to enjoy the show from downtown at The Cut-Off.

Ripley Main Street is inviting the community to join in for what promises to be a memorable evening combining music, food, shopping, and patriotic celebration.
