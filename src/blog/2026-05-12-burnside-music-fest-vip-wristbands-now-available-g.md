# Burnside Music Fest Launches VIP Wristband Pre-Orders for 2026 Event

Organizers of the Burnside Music Fest are now accepting pre-orders for exclusive VIP wristbands ahead of the festival's return to historic downtown Ripley, Mississippi on June 6th, 2026.

## VIP Package Details

The VIP experience is available for $75 per wristband and includes a comprehensive package designed to enhance attendees' festival experience:

- Official festival shirt
- Signed poster from performing artists
- Exclusive meet-and-greet opportunity with festival bands
- Two complimentary drink tickets
- Food vouchers
- Burnside Music Fest branded merchandise and swag

## Limited Availability

Organizers emphasize that only a limited number of VIP wristbands are available, encouraging interested attendees to secure their spots promptly.

## How to Pre-Order

Multiple ordering options are available to accommodate different preferences:

**Digital and Remote Options:**
- Direct message through social media
- Email: ripleymainstreet@gmail.com
- Phone: 662-512-0226
- PayPal: ripleymainstreet@gmail.com
- Venmo: @ripleymainstreet

**In-Person Ordering:**
- Jefferson Street Taproom
- Ripley Main Street office

**Payment Methods:**
Currently accepted payment options include PayPal, Venmo, cash, and check. Festival organizers have announced that an online credit card payment link will be available soon.

## Event Details

The Burnside Music Fest is scheduled for June 6th, 2026, in the historic downtown area of Ripley, Mississippi. The festival celebrates blues and live music in the region.

For more information, follow the event on social media using hashtags #BurnsideMusicFest #RipleyMS #MississippiMusic #BluesMusic #DowntownRipley #LiveMusic.
