# Ripley Main Street Launches Artisan Accelerator Program for Local Makers

Ripley Main Street has announced the launch of the Brown & Covington Artisan Accelerator, an exciting new initiative designed to support and grow local creative talent. The program is now actively recruiting four talented artisans for its inaugural cohort.

## What the Program Offers

Selected participants will gain access to a comprehensive suite of resources to help their creative businesses thrive:

- **Dedicated retail space** to showcase and sell their handmade work
- **Marketing and promotional support** to increase visibility and reach
- **Business development workshops and mentorship** from experienced professionals
- **Opportunities for classes, events, and pop-ups** to engage with the community
- **Customer feedback and sales insights** to inform business decisions
- **A creative community** providing support, collaboration, and networking

## Who Should Apply

The program welcomes both established makers looking to expand their operations and emerging creative entrepreneurs ready to take their businesses to the next level. Whether you work with traditional crafts, contemporary art, handmade goods, or innovative creative ventures, the accelerator aims to provide the infrastructure and support needed for success.

## Application Details

Artisans interested in joining the program are encouraged to submit applications by **August 31, 2026**. Interested candidates can access the application through the provided link or by scanning the QR code on the program announcement.

Ripley Main Street is also calling on community members to help spread the word by sharing this opportunity with local artists, makers, and creative entrepreneurs in their networks.

For more information and to apply, visit: https://app.seemylegacy.com/community/2637/campaign/9513
