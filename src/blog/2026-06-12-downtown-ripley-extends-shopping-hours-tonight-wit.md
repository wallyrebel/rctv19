# Downtown Ripley Extends Hours for After Hours Shopping and Entertainment

Downtown Ripley is extending its summer shopping experience tonight with participating retailers staying open until 7:00 PM as part of the ongoing Downtown After Hours series.

## Evening Plans in Downtown Ripley

Shoppers are invited to take a leisurely stroll around the downtown square this evening, browsing local businesses and enjoying the summer atmosphere. The extended hours provide an opportunity for residents and visitors to support small, locally-owned shops while making the most of a warm evening in the historic district.

## Elvis Bingo Night at The Cut-Off

Following the shopping hours, the evening's entertainment continues at 7:00 PM at The Cut-Off with Elvis Bingo Night. The themed event features Elvis-inspired music bingo with prizes available for winners, adding a fun and festive element to the downtown experience.

## About Downtown After Hours

The Downtown After Hours series runs every Friday in June, offering residents a regular opportunity to engage with their community and support local commerce. The initiative emphasizes the importance of shopping small and staying invested in downtown Ripley's vibrancy.

**Event Details:**
- **Location:** Downtown Ripley
- **Shop Hours:** Open until 7:00 PM
- **Special Event:** Elvis Bingo Night at The Cut-Off at 7:00 PM
- **Frequency:** Every Friday in June

Community organizers encourage attendees to "support local, shop small, and stay awhile" as part of the broader effort to revitalize downtown Ripley.
