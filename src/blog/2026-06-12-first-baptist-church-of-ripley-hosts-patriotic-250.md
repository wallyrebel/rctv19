# First Baptist Church of Ripley Celebrates 250 Years of Freedom with Community Festival

First Baptist Church of Ripley is opening its doors for a special patriotic celebration that promises an evening of entertainment and community togetherness.

## Event Highlights

The "250 Years of Freedom" celebration will feature a diverse lineup of attractions for families of all ages, including:

- **Live Bluegrass Music** - Experience authentic bluegrass performances throughout the evening
- **Food and Refreshments** - Enjoy a variety of food options
- **Games and Activities** - Participate in various games and entertainment
- **Cake Walk** - Try your luck at this classic fundraiser favorite
- **Water Slide** - Cool off with the interactive water slide attraction
- **Fireworks Show** - Cap off the evening with a spectacular fireworks display at dusk

## Community Invitation

The church is welcoming community members to make plans to attend this patriotic gathering. The event combines traditional Americana entertainment with family-friendly activities, making it an ideal outing for residents of all ages.

For complete details and additional event information, attendees are encouraged to consult the official event flyer.

This celebration reflects the church's commitment to bringing the community together while honoring the nation's heritage and independence.
