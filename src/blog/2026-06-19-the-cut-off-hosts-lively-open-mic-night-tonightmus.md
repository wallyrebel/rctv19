## Community Gathers for Evening of Live Music and Entertainment

The Cut-Off, located at 204 S. Commerce Street, is opening its doors tonight for an open mic event that promises to be a highlight of the local entertainment calendar. Running from 7:00 to 9:00 PM, the evening will showcase live performances from area musicians and provide a relaxed setting for community members to gather and enjoy each other's company.

### Easy Sign-Up Process

Participants interested in performing can register online using a QR code or simply arrive at the venue and organizers will accommodate them. The event welcomes singers, instrumentalists, and those who simply want to enjoy the show, making it an inclusive experience for all skill levels and interests.

### Food and Refreshments

Attendees can enjoy food and beverages from local vendors, including Chula's Food Truck and The Gathering Porch Lemonade, ensuring there's something for everyone to enjoy throughout the evening. Guests are encouraged to bring lawn chairs to settle in for the duration of the event.

### Shopping Before the Show

For those looking to make an evening of it, downtown shops will remain open until 7:00 PM, giving visitors an opportunity to browse local retailers before heading to the venue for the musical festivities.

The event represents a community-focused initiative designed to support local talent while fostering connection among neighbors in a festive, music-filled environment.
