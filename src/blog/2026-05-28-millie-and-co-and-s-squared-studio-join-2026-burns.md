# Local Boutiques to Showcase Wares at 2026 Burnside Music Fest

The 2026 Burnside Music Fest is ramping up its vendor lineup with the addition of two popular local boutiques, Millie & Co. and S Squared Studio, organizers announced recently.

## Shopping and Music Combined

Festival attendees will have the opportunity to browse curated collections from both vendors while enjoying a full day of authentic Hill Country Blues music. The vendor booths will feature an eclectic mix of merchandise designed to appeal to diverse shoppers.

## What to Expect

Visitors to the Millie & Co. and S Squared Studio booths can expect to find:

- Stylish boutique clothing and apparel
- Jewelry and accessories
- Home décor items
- Unique gift selections
- And more

## Supporting Local Commerce

The addition of these vendors reinforces the festival's commitment to supporting local businesses in the Ripley, Mississippi area. The event combines entertainment with community-focused shopping, creating a full-day experience for music lovers and retail enthusiasts alike.

The 2026 Burnside Music Fest promises to deliver the perfect blend of live blues music and local shopping, making it a must-visit event for the region.
