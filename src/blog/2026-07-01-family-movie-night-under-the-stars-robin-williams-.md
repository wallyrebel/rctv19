## Community Gathering Brings Classic Comedy to Ripley's Outdoor Screen

The Cut-Off, located at 204 S. Commerce Street in Ripley, Mississippi, is preparing to welcome families for a special outdoor movie night this Friday featuring the beloved Robin Williams comedy "RV."

The event promises a nostalgic evening of entertainment, with the film beginning at dusk. Organizers are encouraging attendees to bring lawn chairs and blankets to make the most of the outdoor viewing experience, creating a relaxed, family-friendly atmosphere perfect for a night under the stars.

### Event Details

**When:** Friday at dusk  
**Where:** The Cut-Off, 204 S. Commerce Street, Ripley, MS  
**What to Bring:** Lawn chairs, blankets, and family members

The movie night is part of a community initiative to bring entertainment to the area, with this month's screening made possible through the generous sponsorship of West Glass Company. The company's support highlights the local business community's commitment to fostering community engagement and family-oriented events.

"RV," released in 2006, follows the misadventures of a family vacation gone awry, making it an ideal choice for a fun, lighthearted evening of entertainment suitable for audiences of all ages. The film's humor and family-centered storyline align perfectly with the community-focused nature of the event.

This outdoor movie screening represents the kind of grassroots entertainment initiative that strengthens community bonds and provides affordable, accessible entertainment options for families in the Ripley area.
