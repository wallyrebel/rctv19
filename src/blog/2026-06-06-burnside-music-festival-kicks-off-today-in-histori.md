# Burnside Music Festival Launches Today in Historic Downtown Ripley

Music fans and food enthusiasts are invited to celebrate the kickoff of the Burnside Music Festival today in Historic Downtown Ripley, with festivities beginning at noon and continuing throughout the day.

## Event Highlights

The festival offers something for everyone:

- **Arts & Crafts Vendors**: Browse local artisan goods from 12 PM to 5 PM
- **Food Trucks**: Vendors will be open starting at noon to satisfy appetites
- **Live Music**: The musical performances begin at 2 PM
- **Rib & Wing Cookoff**: Competition runs all day, promising delicious barbecue offerings

## Free Community Event

One of the best features of today's festival is that admission and parking are completely free, making it an accessible event for the entire community. Whether attendees are looking to enjoy live entertainment, support local artists and food vendors, or participate in the culinary competition, there's no cost to join in the festivities.

The festival promises to be a vibrant celebration of music, community, and local culture in the heart of Historic Downtown Ripley.
