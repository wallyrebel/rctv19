# West Glass Co. Recognized as VIP Sponsor of Burnside Music Festival

West Glass Co. has earned recognition as a VIP Tent Sponsor for the Burnside Music Festival, demonstrating its commitment to supporting community events and cultural celebrations in the Ripley, Mississippi area.

## A Century of Celebration

The sponsorship comes as the festival marks a significant milestone—100 years of the Burnside legacy. The event, set to take place in Historic Downtown Ripley, aims to bring together residents and visitors for a celebration of music, community spirit, and entertainment.

## Community Investment

Organizers of the Burnside Music Festival expressed their gratitude for West Glass Co.'s investment in the event and the broader community. The company's VIP sponsorship plays a crucial role in enabling the festival to deliver quality programming and experiences for attendees.

"Their support helps us celebrate 100 Years of the Burnside Legacy and bring great music, community, and fun to Historic Downtown Ripley," organizers stated in announcing the partnership.

## Festival Details

The Burnside Music Festival is scheduled to take place on Saturday, promising attendees live music performances and community activities throughout the day. The event continues a tradition of bringing entertainment and cultural programming to downtown Ripley.

The festival's success depends on partnerships with local businesses like West Glass Co., whose sponsorship helps make the celebration possible for the community.
