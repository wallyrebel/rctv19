# MLK Dream Team Launches First Juneteenth Freedom Day Celebration

The MLK Dream Team is bringing the community together for a special inaugural Juneteenth Freedom Day celebration that promises to be a memorable day of cultural celebration and community connection.

## Event Highlights

The celebration will feature a diverse lineup of entertainment and activities for all ages:

- **Live Entertainment**: Performance by For Appeal
- **Music**: DJ Gucci Girl spinning tracks throughout the day
- **Food & Merchandise**: Multiple vendors offering food and merchandise
- **Family Activities**: Face painting, bouncy house, and various activities designed for the whole family

Organizers recommend attendees bring lawn chairs to enjoy the festivities comfortably.

## Event Details

**Location:**
Ripley Main Street
205 W Spring Street
Ripley, MS 38663

## Pre-Event Merchandise

The MLK Dream Team is building excitement for the celebration by offering commemorative t-shirts available for pre-order. Community members can reserve shirts by contacting MLK team members directly. The pre-order deadline is set for the beginning of June.

## Community Impact

This inaugural event reflects the organization's commitment to creating meaningful opportunities that bring the community together while honoring the historical significance of Juneteenth. The celebration serves as a platform to recognize important cultural heritage and build stronger community bonds.

For more information about the event, interested attendees are encouraged to connect with MLK Dream Team members directly.
