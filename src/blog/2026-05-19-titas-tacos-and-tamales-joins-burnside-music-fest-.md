# Tita's Tacos & Tamales Brings Authentic Flavors to Burnside Music Fest

The Burnside Music Fest is ramping up its vendor lineup with the addition of Tita's Tacos & Tamales, a beloved local establishment that promises to bring authentic street food to the Historic Downtown Ripley event on Saturday, June 6, 2026.

## A Local Favorite Joins the Celebration

Organizers are excited to welcome Tita's Tacos & Tamales to the festival, recognizing the vendor as a community staple known for quality, authentic Mexican cuisine. The food vendor will offer festival attendees a chance to enjoy traditional street tacos, homemade tamales, and other fresh-made favorites that have earned the establishment its local reputation.

## Supporting Local While Enjoying the Festival

The addition of Tita's Tacos & Tamales underscores the Burnside Music Fest's commitment to supporting local vendors and businesses. Festival-goers are encouraged to come hungry and make the food vendor a must-stop destination while enjoying live music performances throughout the day.

"Come hungry and stop by for authentic street tacos, tamales, and fresh-made favorites from a local favorite you won't want to miss," organizers stated in their vendor spotlight announcement. "Grab some amazing food, enjoy great music, and support local vendors while you're there!"

## Mark Your Calendar

The Burnside Music Fest takes place on Saturday, June 6, 2026, in Historic Downtown Ripley. Food vendors like Tita's Tacos & Tamales will be ready to serve hungry festival attendees looking for quality local cuisine and authentic flavors.
