# Burnside Music Festival Brings Live Music to Ripley This Saturday

Music fans in Ripley, Mississippi are in for a treat this Saturday as the Burnside Music Festival takes over Historic Downtown Ripley with a stellar lineup of local and regional artists.

## Festival Details

The festival opens its doors at noon, with live musical performances commencing at 2:00 PM. The event promises an afternoon and evening of entertainment celebrating the rich musical heritage of the Burnside family.

## Complete Lineup

The festival will feature eight acts throughout the day:

- Kody Shade Harrell
- Pistol and the Queen
- The Pip Project
- Titus Gillard
- Cedric Burnside
- Lena Beach featuring Boo Mitchell
- Garry Burnside
- Jam Session (to follow the main performances)

## What to Expect

With a mix of established and emerging artists, the Burnside Music Festival celebrates the legacy of blues and roots music that has deep connections to the region. The inclusion of a jam session at the end of the night promises spontaneous musical collaboration and interaction between performers.

The event takes place in the heart of Historic Downtown Ripley, making it easily accessible for music lovers throughout the region. Whether you're a longtime blues enthusiast or simply looking for a Saturday afternoon of live entertainment, the Burnside Music Festival offers something for everyone.

For more information about the festival, follow #BurnsideMusicFest on social media.
