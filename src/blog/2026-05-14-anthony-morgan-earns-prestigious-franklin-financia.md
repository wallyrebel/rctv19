# Anthony Morgan Named 1st Franklin Financial Tippah County Sports Male Athlete of the Year

Anthony Morgan has earned one of Tippah County's most prestigious athletic honors, being selected as the 2025-2026 1st Franklin Financial Tippah County Sports Male Athlete of the Year.

The award, presented by Franklin Financial, recognizes outstanding athletic performance and achievement among male athletes competing in Tippah County schools and organizations. Morgan's selection highlights his exceptional contributions to his sport and his dedication to athletic excellence throughout the season.

This honor places Morgan among the county's elite athletes and celebrates his hard work, skill, and commitment to his team. The recognition comes as a testament to his performance on the field or court and his impact on the local sports community.

Morgan joins a distinguished group of athletes who have previously received this award, further cementing his legacy as one of Tippah County's most accomplished competitors.
