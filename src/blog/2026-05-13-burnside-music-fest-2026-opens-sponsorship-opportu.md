# Burnside Music Fest Seeks Community Sponsors for 2026 Celebration

The Burnside Music Fest is opening its doors to new sponsors as organizers prepare for the upcoming 2026 event celebrating Mississippi's rich musical legacy in Historic Downtown Ripley.

With several businesses and community supporters already committed to the festival, organizers are encouraging additional sponsors to join the effort to create a vibrant, welcoming community music experience.

## Sponsorship Levels Available

Two sponsorship tiers are currently available:

- **VIP Tent Sponsor**: $500
- **Burnside Music Fest Sponsor**: $250

## Benefits of Sponsorship

Businesses and organizations that become sponsors will receive comprehensive recognition including:

- Event signage and promotional materials
- Social media promotion
- Placement on the official event website
- Stage shoutouts during the festival
- Exclusive VIP perks and benefits
- Additional promotional opportunities

## Supporting Local Music and Downtown Development

According to event organizers, sponsorships play a crucial role in sustaining the festival while supporting the revitalization of downtown Ripley. The event celebrates the area's connection to blues music history while fostering community engagement through live entertainment.

"Thank you for supporting live music, local events, and downtown Ripley," organizers stated in their sponsorship announcement.

Interested businesses and organizations can find more information and complete their sponsorship registration through the official event website. Those wishing to participate are encouraged to act soon, as sponsorship opportunities may be limited.

For additional details and to sign up as a sponsor, interested parties can visit the Burnside Music Fest sponsorship portal.
