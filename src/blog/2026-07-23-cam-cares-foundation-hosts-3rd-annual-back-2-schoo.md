# Cam Cares Foundation Brings Back 3rd Annual Back 2 School Giveaway

The Cam Cares Foundation is returning this Saturday with its highly anticipated 3rd Annual Back 2 School Giveaway, bringing essential resources and support to local students as they prepare for the upcoming school year.

## Event Details

The giveaway will take place at The Cut-Off from **11:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.** this Saturday. The event is free and open to all students in the community.

## What's Available

Attendees can take advantage of multiple offerings designed to help students start the school year prepared:

- **Free school supplies** for students of all ages
- **Hygiene products** to support student wellness
- **Access to the Blessings Closet**, featuring free clothing and shoes for students in need
- **Free snow cone vouchers** for every student attending

## Community Support

The foundation expressed gratitude to everyone involved in making the event possible, emphasizing the community's commitment to supporting local students during this important transition.

"Thank you to everyone making this event possible and supporting our local students as they head back to school!" the foundation stated.

With back-to-school season in full swing, this giveaway provides crucial assistance to families seeking to equip their children with necessary supplies and clothing as they enter the classroom.
