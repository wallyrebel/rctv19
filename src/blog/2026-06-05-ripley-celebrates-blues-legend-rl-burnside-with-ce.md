# Ripley Celebrates Blues Legend R.L. Burnside with Centennial Music Festival This Weekend

Ripley is gearing up for a musical celebration this weekend as the Burnside Music Festival makes its return to Main Street with an extra-special lineup honoring the centennial birthday of blues legend R.L. Burnside.

The festival promises to be a weekend-long tribute to the influential Hill Country Blues pioneer, featuring live music performances from talented musicians carrying on the blues tradition. Beyond the musical performances, attendees can expect authentic BBQ and additional entertainment throughout the event.

R.L. Burnside, a towering figure in American blues history, left an indelible mark on the Hill Country Blues genre with his distinctive guitar style and soulful performances. The 100th birthday celebration serves as a testament to his enduring legacy and continued influence on blues music.

The festival represents an important cultural event for the Ripley community, drawing blues enthusiasts and music lovers to celebrate both the genre and the man who helped shape it. With live performances and traditional food, the Burnside Music Festival offers visitors an immersive experience into the heart of blues culture.

For those planning to attend, the festival will take place this weekend on Ripley's Main Street. Those interested in experiencing authentic Hill Country Blues and paying tribute to a music legend should mark their calendars for what promises to be a memorable celebration of blues heritage.
