# Burnside Music Fest Unveils Premium VIP Experience Ahead of June 6 Event

The Burnside Music Fest is rolling out an enhanced festival experience with the introduction of a limited VIP Tent package designed to elevate attendees' enjoyment of the upcoming event.

## VIP Package Details

VIP wristbands are available for $75 in pre-order and come packed with exclusive perks:

- Festival t-shirt
- Signed poster from performing artists
- Meet-and-greet access with festival bands
- Food provisions
- Two drink tickets
- Exclusive Burnside Music Fest merchandise

## Limited Availability

Organizers emphasize that VIP wristbands are limited in quantity, urging interested festival-goers to secure their packages before they sell out.

## How to Pre-Order

Prospective VIP attendees have multiple convenient options to purchase their wristbands:

- **Scan the QR code** featured in promotional materials
- **Visit the online link** at https://app.seemylegacy.com/community/2637/campaign/9093
- **Direct message** the festival organizers on social media platforms
- **Visit in person** at Jefferson Street Taproom

## Event Details

The Burnside Music Fest is scheduled for June 6 in downtown Ripley, promising a full day of live music and entertainment for all attendees.
