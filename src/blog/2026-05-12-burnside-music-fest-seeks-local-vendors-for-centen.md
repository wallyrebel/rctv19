# Burnside Music Fest Opens Vendor Applications for Historic Blues Celebration

Ripley, Mississippi is preparing to celebrate the centennial of blues legend R.L. Burnside with an exciting community festival that organizers hope will showcase the region's vibrant local talent and entrepreneurial spirit.

## Festival Details

The Burnside Music Fest will take place on Saturday, June 6th, 2026, in Historic Downtown Ripley, bringing together live music, local culture, food, shopping, and community engagement in the heart of the city.

## Vendor Opportunity

Organizers are actively recruiting local vendors and makers to participate in the celebration. The festival seeks:

- Handmade goods and artisan products
- Local art and crafts
- Small businesses and independent makers

Vendor spaces will be set up in a pop-up area outside the Blues Alley, providing high foot traffic and visibility for participating businesses.

## How to Apply

The vendor fee is just $30 per space, making it an affordable opportunity for small business owners and makers to reach a community-focused audience. Interested vendors can apply by scanning the QR code on the festival flyer or by contacting Ripley Main Street directly for additional information.

**Important note:** Festival organizers emphasize that spaces are limited, so vendors are encouraged to reserve their spots soon.

This grassroots approach to vendor recruitment reflects the festival's commitment to supporting local commerce while honoring one of blues music's most influential figures. For those interested in participating, applications are being accepted now through the official festival channels.
