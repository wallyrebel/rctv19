# Shake It Up Ice Cream Brings Sweet Treats to Burnside Music Festival

The Burnside Music Festival is heating up this summer, and one local vendor is helping festival-goers cool down with delicious frozen desserts.

## Festival Details

Shake It Up Ice Cream will operate a booth at the centennial celebration, which takes place **Saturday, June 6** at **Blues Alley** in Historic Downtown Ripley, Mississippi. The vendor is among several businesses participating in this year's special event marking 100 years of the Burnside Legacy.

## What to Expect

Festival attendees can look forward to a full day of entertainment and community engagement, featuring live music performances, diverse food vendors, and family-friendly activities throughout Historic Downtown Ripley. The addition of Shake It Up Ice Cream provides the perfect complement to an afternoon or evening of outdoor music enjoyment.

## Supporting Local Business

Organizers expressed enthusiasm about the partnership, highlighting the importance of local vendors in creating memorable community events. The inclusion of Shake It Up Ice Cream underscores the festival's commitment to supporting neighborhood businesses while enhancing the overall attendee experience.

Whether you're a longtime fan of the Burnside Legacy or new to the celebration, be sure to stop by the Shake It Up Ice Cream booth for a refreshing treat while enjoying the festival's musical lineup.
