The citizens of Ripley welcomed 2026 with a spectacular New Year's Eve celebration in downtown, capping off a memorable year for our community.

## Evening Festivities

The celebration began at 7 PM with activities for the whole family:

- **Kids' Zone** featuring games, face painting, and crafts
- **Live Music** from local band Southern Comfort and the Ripley High School Jazz Ensemble
- **Food Vendors** serving favorites from local restaurants
- **Hot Cocoa Station** sponsored by the Ripley Lions Club

## Countdown to Midnight

As midnight approached, Mayor Thompson took the stage to reflect on the past year's achievements and express optimism for the year ahead.

"2025 was a year of growth and progress for Ripley," Mayor Thompson said. "As we enter 2026, let's continue working together to make our community even stronger."

The crowd of several hundred then counted down to midnight, celebrating with a fireworks display over the courthouse.

## Community Reflections

For many families, the New Year's Eve gathering has become an annual tradition.

"We've been coming to this celebration for ten years," said resident Maria Gonzalez. "It's a wonderful way to celebrate with our neighbors and start the new year with positive energy."

## 2026 Outlook

The new year brings several exciting developments for Ripley:

- **Downtown development project** breaking ground in spring
- **New community center expansion** opening in fall
- **150th anniversary celebrations** for the City of Ripley

## Thank You

Special thanks to the organizing committee, volunteers, sponsors, and everyone who attended. Your participation makes these community events possible.
