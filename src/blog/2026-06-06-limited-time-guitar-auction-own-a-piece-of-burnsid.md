## Burnside Festival Launches Exclusive Guitar Auction with Free U.S. Shipping

Music fans and collectors have just hours remaining to bid on an exclusive piece of Burnside Festival memorabilia: a guitar signed by the festival's 2025 lineup of performers.

### Online Auction Closing Tonight

The online bidding window is rapidly closing, with bids accepted until 10:00 PM tonight. Interested bidders can place their offers through the festival's official Facebook auction page, accessible via QR code or direct link.

For those unable to attend the festival in person, the winning bidder will receive complimentary shipping to any location within the United States—removing geographical barriers to acquiring this one-of-a-kind instrument.

### Live Auction at Tonight's Festival

Beyond the online opportunity, the Burnside Festival will host a live auction for a second signed guitar during tonight's event. This guitar has been autographed by the festival's 2026 performer lineup, offering attendees an additional chance to secure a piece of festival history.

### Celebrating Hill Country Blues Legacy

These signed guitars represent more than just musical instruments; they embody the rich heritage of Hill Country Blues and the Burnside Festival's commitment to honoring this musical tradition. Whether acquiring one as a collector's item, a gift for a devoted music lover, or a memento of the festival itself, these guitars offer a tangible connection to the artists and legacy the event celebrates.

With limited availability and time running out for online bidders, interested parties are encouraged to act quickly to secure their bid before the 10 PM deadline.
