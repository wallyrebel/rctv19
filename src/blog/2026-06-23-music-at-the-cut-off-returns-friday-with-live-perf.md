# Music at The Cut-Off Returns Friday with Live Performance and Fireworks

Downtown Ripley is gearing up for a festive summer evening this Friday, June 26, as Music at The Cut-Off makes its return to the community. The event promises a laid-back atmosphere perfect for kicking off the weekend with friends and family.

## What to Expect

The evening will feature a variety of activities for visitors of all ages. Local businesses will remain open for shopping until 7:00 PM, giving residents an opportunity to support downtown merchants while enjoying the summer weather. Live music from Trevor Wilson will set the tone for the evening at The Cut-Off, creating an ideal backdrop for a relaxing night out.

Attendees are encouraged to bring lawn chairs to settle in comfortably while enjoying the music. The venue will also offer dining options, allowing guests to grab dinner and make an evening of it downtown.

## Fireworks to Follow

The festivities will continue well into the evening as the City of Ripley Fireworks Show begins at 9:30 PM near Ripley High School. Those attending Music at The Cut-Off will have the option to head to the official viewing area or simply enjoy the fireworks display from downtown after the musical performance concludes.

## Community Celebration

Organizers are promoting the event as an ideal way to celebrate the start of the weekend while supporting local businesses and enjoying community spirit. The combination of live entertainment, shopping opportunities, dining, and fireworks creates a comprehensive summer experience for downtown Ripley residents.
