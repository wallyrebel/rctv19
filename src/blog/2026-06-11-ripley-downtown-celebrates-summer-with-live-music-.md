# Downtown Ripley Sets Stage for Summer Celebration

The City of Ripley is calling residents and visitors to mark their calendars for a vibrant evening of entertainment and community spirit on **Friday, June 26**.

## What to Expect

The event promises a full lineup of attractions designed to kick off the weekend in style:

### Shopping and Entertainment
Downtown merchants will extend their hours for an **After Hours shopping experience**, giving attendees the opportunity to browse local boutiques and businesses while enjoying the evening atmosphere.

### Live Music
Music lovers can look forward to a live performance from **Trevor Wilson at The Cut-Off**, bringing entertainment to the heart of downtown Ripley.

### Grand Finale
The evening will culminate with the **City of Ripley's Fireworks Show at 9:30 PM**, which will be launched near Ripley High School, providing a spectacular conclusion to the night.

## Community Connection

Organizers describe the event as "the perfect way to kick off the weekend with great music, local shopping, and a community celebration," emphasizing the blend of entertainment, local commerce, and civic pride that defines the occasion.

Residents are encouraged to gather downtown and make the most of this special evening that celebrates what makes Ripley's downtown district unique.
