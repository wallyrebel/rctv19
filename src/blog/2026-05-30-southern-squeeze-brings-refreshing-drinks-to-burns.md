# Southern Squeeze to Quench Your Thirst at Burnside Music Fest

Festival attendees looking for the perfect beverage to complement a day of live music have a new destination: Southern Squeeze will be among the vendors at the upcoming Burnside Music Fest in historic downtown Ripley.

## Cool Drinks for a Hot Day

The vendor will be offering a curated selection of refreshing beverages designed to keep guests cool throughout the day. Their menu features:

- Fresh lemonades
- Dirty sodas
- Specialty drinks

## Event Details

The Burnside Music Fest takes place on **Saturday, June 6, 2026**, in the charming historic downtown area of Ripley, Mississippi. The event celebrates Hill Country blues, providing the perfect backdrop for enjoying quality local beverages.

## Shop Local, Drink Local

By stopping at Southern Squeeze, festival attendees support local businesses while enjoying quality refreshments. The vendor invites music fans to grab a drink, settle in, and experience a day of great music and community spirit in one of the region's most historic downtown districts.

Whether you're a blues enthusiast or simply looking for a great day out, Southern Squeeze promises to be a highlight of your Burnside Music Fest experience.
