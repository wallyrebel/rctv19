# Gathering Porch Brings Refreshing Beverages to Burnside Music Fest

The Burnside Music Fest is thrilled to announce that Gathering Porch will be joining the vendor lineup for the 2026 festival, scheduled for Saturday, June 6th in Historic Downtown Ripley, Mississippi.

## What to Expect

Visitors to the festival can stop by the Gathering Porch booth to enjoy:

- **Fresh Lemonade** - A classic refreshment perfect for warm festival days
- **Dirty Sodas** - A trendy beverage option that's become increasingly popular at community events

## Festival Details

The Burnside Music Fest promises a full day of entertainment and local flavor, featuring live music performances, food trucks, and a variety of local vendors. With activities and attractions throughout the day, attendees will have plenty of opportunities to cool off with a refreshing drink from Gathering Porch.

## Supporting Local Business

The festival organizers encourage attendees to show their support for local businesses like Gathering Porch. By choosing to purchase from local vendors, festival-goers directly contribute to the economic vitality of the Ripley community and help sustain small businesses.

Mark your calendars for June 6th and plan to visit Gathering Porch at the Burnside Music Fest for a refreshing break during a day of music and community celebration.
