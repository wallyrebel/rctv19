# Your Complete Guide to the Burnside Music Festival: Parking, Stages, and More

Planning to attend the Burnside Music Festival this Saturday? Festival organizers want to make sure you have all the information you need for a smooth and enjoyable experience.

## Festival Map Now Available

The festival has released a comprehensive map designed to help attendees navigate Historic Downtown Ripley with ease. Whether you're arriving early or fashionably late, the map highlights everything you need to know about the event's layout and logistics.

## What You'll Find on the Map

The detailed guide includes:

- **Parking locations** for convenient vehicle access
- **Main stage** and performance areas
- **VIP tent** for premium attendees
- **Food trucks** and dining options
- **Vendor booths** featuring local and regional businesses
- **BBQ Cookoff** competition area
- Additional amenities and facilities

## Plan Ahead and Share

Festival organizers recommend saving the map to your phone for easy reference throughout the day. The map is also shareable, making it simple to coordinate with friends and family planning to attend.

## Celebrating a Century of Music and Heritage

This year's festival marks a special milestone—100 years of the Burnside Legacy. Taking place in the heart of Historic Downtown Ripley, the event promises live music, delicious food, and community celebration.

With parking clearly marked and all major attractions identified on the map, attendees can focus on what matters most: enjoying great music, food, and company. Don't forget to share the map with anyone joining you at the festival!
