# Elvis Bingo Comes to Downtown Ripley: A Rock 'n' Roll Twist on a Classic Game

Downtown Ripley is getting ready to shake things up this Friday with a musical take on a timeless game. Elvis Bingo, hosted by Ripley Main Street at The Cut-Off, promises an evening of entertainment, prizes, and plenty of rock 'n' roll spirit.

## How It Works

Elvis Bingo operates on a simple premise: instead of traditional numbered bingo cards, players receive cards filled with Elvis song titles. As classic Elvis hits play throughout the evening, participants listen carefully and mark off songs from their cards. The first player to complete five in a row and shout "BINGO!" wins a prize.

"No Elvis expertise required," organizers emphasized in their announcement. The event focuses on fun and community enjoyment rather than deep knowledge of the King of Rock 'n' Roll's extensive catalog.

## What to Expect

The evening will feature:

- **Classic Elvis Hits**: A curated selection of the legendary performer's most recognizable songs
- **Multiple Prize Opportunities**: Prizes will be awarded throughout the night, not just for winning bingo
- **Costume Incentives**: Those who dress as Elvis receive a complimentary prize, whether they choose young Elvis, Vegas Elvis, Jailhouse Rock Elvis, or any other iconic version
- **Social Atmosphere**: The event welcomes groups of friends and family looking for a fun night out

## Event Details

**Location**: The Cut-Off, Downtown Ripley  
**Date**: Friday, June 12  
**Time**: 7:00 PM

Organizers promise that whether attendees come in full Elvis regalia or casual attire, "you're guaranteed to have a good time." The event combines the familiar gameplay of bingo with the timeless appeal of Elvis's music, creating a unique entertainment experience for the Ripley community.

For more information, community members can follow Ripley Main Street's social media channels.
