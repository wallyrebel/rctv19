## Burnside Festival Guitar Auction Launches to Support Community Revitalization

A unique opportunity to own a piece of Hill Country Blues history is now available as a signed guitar from the 2025 Burnside Music Festival goes under the hammer in an online auction.

The instrument, autographed by the artists who performed at the festival, is being auctioned to benefit Historic Downtown Ripley revitalization projects. The initiative combines support for live music with community development efforts in Ripley, Mississippi.

### Auction Details

**Starting Bid:** $100
**Minimum Raise:** $10
**Auction Deadline:** Saturday, June 6 at 10:00 PM

Prospective bidders can place their offers directly in the comments section of the auction post. The highest bid at the time the auction closes will secure the guitar.

### Supporting Local Music and Community

All proceeds from the sale will be directed toward downtown revitalization initiatives, making this auction a meaningful way for music enthusiasts and community supporters to contribute to Ripley's development while acquiring a memorable collectible.

The Burnside Music Festival organizers emphasize that this auction represents an opportunity to support live music, Hill Country Blues traditions, and the local community simultaneously.

Interested bidders are encouraged to place their bids before the Saturday deadline to participate in this charitable fundraising effort.
