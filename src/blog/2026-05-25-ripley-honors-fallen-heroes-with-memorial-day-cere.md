# Ripley Honors Fallen Heroes with Memorial Day Ceremony and Historical Marker Unveiling

As the nation pauses to remember and honor those who made the ultimate sacrifice for our country, the city of Ripley is hosting a meaningful Memorial Day program to pay tribute to fallen service members.

The community is invited to attend the Ripley Memorial Day program this morning at the Ripley Sportsplex, beginning at 10:00 a.m. The ceremony will feature several meaningful components designed to honor veterans and active military personnel.

## Program Highlights

The morning's events will include:

- **Historical Marker Unveiling**: The Daughters of the American Revolution will unveil a historical marker as part of the ceremony
- **American Legion Program**: Representatives from the American Legion will deliver remarks and participate in the observance
- **Refreshments**: Following the formal program, refreshments will be provided for attendees

## A Call to Reflect and Remember

Organizers are encouraging all community members to take a moment to reflect on the sacrifices made by those who served their country. The program provides an opportunity for residents to gather together in solidarity and express their gratitude to the men and women of the armed forces.

Memorial Day, observed on the last Monday of May, serves as a solemn occasion for Americans to remember and honor military personnel who died while serving in the U.S. Armed Forces. For Ripley residents, this year's program at the Sportsplex offers a meaningful way to participate in this important national observance.
