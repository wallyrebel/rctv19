# Ripley Public Library Launches Book Bingo Week to Inspire Young Readers

The Ripley Public Library is rolling out an exciting initiative designed to get children excited about reading this week. Book Bingo, a fun and interactive program, invites young readers of all levels to participate in a week-long celebration of literature and literacy.

## Event Details

**When:** Monday through Friday

**Time:** 11:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m. daily

**Where:** Ripley Public Library, 308 North Commerce Street, Ripley

## What to Expect

Participants will enjoy playing Book Bingo while exploring great stories, with the chance to win books as prizes. The program is designed to appeal to a wide range of young readers, whether they are seasoned book enthusiasts or just beginning their reading journey.

Beyond the competitive element of the game, Book Bingo offers children an opportunity to make new friends, discover titles they might not have encountered otherwise, and develop a deeper appreciation for reading. Library organizers emphasize that the program welcomes readers at all levels, making it an inclusive event for the entire community.

As libraries continue to seek innovative ways to promote literacy and foster a love of reading among young people, initiatives like Book Bingo serve as valuable tools for engagement and learning. Parents and guardians looking for screen-free activities and educational entertainment are encouraged to bring their children to participate in this week-long event.
