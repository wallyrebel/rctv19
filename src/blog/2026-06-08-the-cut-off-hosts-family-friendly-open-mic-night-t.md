# The Cut-Off Brings Live Entertainment to Downtown Ripley

The Cut-Off is opening its doors for an open mic night this Thursday, inviting singers, musicians, poets, comedians, and performers of all kinds to showcase their talents in a welcoming, family-friendly environment.

## Event Details

**Date:** Thursday, June 19  
**Time:** 7:00–9:00 PM  
**Location:** The Cut-Off, 204 S. Commerce Street, Downtown Ripley

## How to Participate

Aspiring performers can secure a time slot by scanning a QR code to register in advance. The venue will support Bluetooth connectivity, allowing musicians to bring their own backing tracks or play original compositions.

## Extended Downtown Experience

The open mic night is part of the broader Downtown After Hours series, a community initiative designed to energize the downtown area. Participating downtown retailers will extend their hours until 7:00 PM on the remaining Fridays in June, offering residents additional opportunities to shop, dine, and enjoy the local business community.

## Community Focus

Organizers emphasize the event's role in celebrating local talent while fostering community connection during the summer season. The casual, inclusive format welcomes both experienced performers and those sharing their talents for the first time.

Interested participants are encouraged to sign up early to secure their preferred performance slot.
