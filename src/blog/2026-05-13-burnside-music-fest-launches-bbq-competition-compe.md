## BBQ Enthusiasts Invited to Compete at Burnside Music Fest Cook-Off

The Burnside Music Fest is calling all barbecue masters to showcase their culinary skills at an upcoming cook-off competition scheduled for June 6th in Historic Downtown Ripley, Mississippi.

### Competition Details

The festival will feature two competitive categories:

- **Ribs Category** — $100 entry fee
- **Chicken Wings Category** — $50 entry fee

Prizes will be awarded in both divisions, giving competitors the opportunity to earn recognition and cash rewards for their smoked meat expertise.

### How to Participate

Interested contestants can pick up their meat anytime after 8:00 AM at Freemans & Co., the official sponsor of the cook-off. Organizers have specified that **only wood smokers will be considered for judging**, ensuring traditional barbecue preparation methods are used throughout the competition.

Aspiring competitors can register through the official entry link available on the Burnside Music Fest website.

### A Full Day of Entertainment

Beyond the competitive cooking, the festival promises a comprehensive day of community celebration featuring live music performances, food vendors, and downtown shopping opportunities. The event emphasizes celebrating Mississippi's rich musical heritage while supporting local businesses and artisans.

### Get More Information

For complete festival details, updates, announcements, sponsorship opportunities, and vendor information, visitors are encouraged to check out **BurnsideMusicFest.com**.

The Burnside Music Fest cook-off represents a unique opportunity for barbecue enthusiasts to compete, celebrate Mississippi culture, and connect with their community.
