# Blue Mountain Christian's Riddle Secures Top-15 Finish at NAIA National Golf Championship

Sophomore Cobey Riddle of Blue Mountain Christian University has delivered an impressive performance at the NAIA Men's Golf National Championship, finishing tied for 15th place in the individual standings.

Riddle's strong showing at the national tournament highlights the promising talent within Blue Mountain Christian's golf program. The sophomore's consistent play throughout the championship rounds helped position him among the nation's elite amateur golfers competing at the NAIA level.

His top-15 finish represents a significant achievement for both Riddle individually and for Blue Mountain Christian University's golf program as a whole. The national championship provides one of the most competitive stages in NAIA athletics, attracting the best collegiate golfers from institutions across the country.

Riddle's performance demonstrates the depth of talent on the Blue Mountain Christian roster and bodes well for the program's future aspirations. As a sophomore, he has established himself as a key contributor to the Eagles' national championship efforts.

The NAIA Men's Golf National Championship serves as the premier event for determining the nation's top collegiate golfers, making Riddle's top-15 finish a notable accomplishment in his college golf career.
