# Local Healthcare Provider Backs Burnside Music Fest

North Mississippi Primary Health Care has stepped up as a sponsor of the Burnside Music Fest, demonstrating a strong commitment to supporting local culture and community events in the Ripley area.

## Community Partnership in Action

The sponsorship represents more than just financial support—it reflects a broader dedication to preserving and celebrating the rich musical heritage of the North Mississippi Hill Country. Through partnerships like this, organizers say they can continue bringing vibrant live music experiences and cultural tourism to the heart of historic downtown Ripley.

## About the Festival

Burnside Music Fest is scheduled for Saturday, June 6, in downtown Ripley. The event celebrates the region's legendary blues tradition while energizing the community and attracting visitors to the area.

## Supporting Local Culture

Organizers expressed their gratitude for the healthcare provider's involvement, noting that community partnerships are essential for sustaining local events and preserving cultural traditions. By backing the festival, North Mississippi Primary Health Care is helping ensure that the distinctive sounds and stories of Hill Country blues continue to resonate with both longtime fans and new audiences.

Festival organizers encourage residents and visitors to mark their calendars for the June 6 celebration of music, community, and local pride.
