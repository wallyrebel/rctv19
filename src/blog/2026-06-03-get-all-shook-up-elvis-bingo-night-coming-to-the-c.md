# Elvis Bingo Night Returns to Downtown Ripley

Fans of the King of Rock and Roll are invited to lace up their blue suede shoes and head to The Cut-Off for an evening of Elvis-themed entertainment and games.

## Event Details

The Cut-Off, located at 204 S. Commerce Street in downtown Ripley, will host the special bingo event on **Thursday, June 12 at 7:00 PM**. The evening promises to be filled with classic Elvis music, themed fun, and plenty of opportunities to win prizes.

## What to Expect

Attendees can look forward to:

- **Multiple prize giveaways** throughout the night
- **Bingo games** designed for players of all ages
- **Classic Elvis music** and entertainment
- **Themed atmosphere** celebrating the legendary performer

## Who Should Attend

The event is **free to play** and welcomes everyone—families, friends, and Elvis enthusiasts of all ages are encouraged to attend. Organizers are inviting guests to "bring your friends, bring your family, and come see if you can leave all shook up... with a prize in hand!"

For more information, follow Ripley Main Street on social media or visit The Cut-Off directly.
