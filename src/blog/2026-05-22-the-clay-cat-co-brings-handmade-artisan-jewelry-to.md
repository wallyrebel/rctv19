# The Clay Cat Co. Joins Burnside Music Fest Vendor Lineup

The Clay Cat Co., a local artisan specializing in handmade polymer clay jewelry, will be setting up shop at the upcoming Burnside Music Fest this Saturday, June 6, in historic downtown Ripley.

## A Full Day of Music, Food, and Local Shopping

Festival attendees will have the opportunity to visit The Clay Cat Co.'s booth to browse and purchase unique handmade polymer clay earrings, jewelry, and other creative pieces. The vendor spotlight comes as part of a larger celebration featuring live music performances, food vendors, and local shopping opportunities throughout the day.

## Supporting Hill Country's Creative Community

The event promises to deliver an authentic Hill Country experience, with live blues music serving as the backdrop for a community-focused shopping and entertainment experience. Organizers are encouraging festival-goers to make a point of supporting The Clay Cat Co. and other local vendors featured at the event.

"Stop by their booth to shop handmade polymer clay earrings, jewelry, and more while enjoying a full day of live music, food, shopping, and Hill Country blues," event organizers stated in their announcement.

The Burnside Music Fest represents an opportunity for both visitors and locals to discover talented artisans while celebrating the region's vibrant music and cultural heritage.
