# Heat & Wellness Recovery Spa Joins Ripley's Main Street Moolah Program

Ripley Main Street announced the addition of Heat & Wellness Recovery Spa to its growing roster of participating businesses accepting Main Street Moolah, the community's local currency initiative.

The expansion brings wellness and recovery services into the Main Street Moolah ecosystem, allowing residents to direct their local currency toward relaxation and health-focused treatments while simultaneously supporting a local business.

## Supporting Community Commerce

Main Street Moolah operates as a community investment tool designed to keep dollars circulating within Ripley's local economy. By accepting the currency, Heat & Wellness Recovery Spa joins other local merchants in strengthening the town's commercial district and fostering economic resilience.

"We're excited to welcome Heat & Wellness Recovery Spa to the growing list of businesses that accept Ripley Main Street Moolah," organizers stated. "Now you can use your Main Street Moolah toward relaxation, recovery, and wellness while supporting a local business."

## How to Participate

Residents interested in purchasing Main Street Moolah can order through the program's official platform. The initiative encourages community members to shop locally and invest directly in Ripley's economic development.

Program organizers emphasized gratitude for community participation, stating: "Thank you for shopping local and investing in our community!"

For more information about Main Street Moolah or to place an order, residents can visit the program's official ordering page.
