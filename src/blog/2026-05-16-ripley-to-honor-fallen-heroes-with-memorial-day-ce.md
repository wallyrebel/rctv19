# Ripley Invites Community to Honor Fallen Heroes at Memorial Day Ceremony

Ripley, Mississippi will come together on Monday, May 25, 2026, to remember and pay tribute to the brave men and women who gave their lives in service to the United States.

## Event Details

The Ripley Memorial Day Program will take place at 10:00 a.m. at the Ripley Sportsplex. The community is invited to join in this solemn observance that will feature multiple components honoring those who made the ultimate sacrifice.

## Program Highlights

The morning's ceremony will include:

- **American Legion Program**: A formal presentation by the American Legion
- **D.A.R. Historical Marker Unveiling**: The Daughters of the American Revolution will unveil a historical marker
- **Light Refreshments**: Fellowship and light refreshments will follow the ceremony

## A Time for Reflection

As Memorial Day approaches, organizers are encouraging residents to take a moment to reflect on the freedoms secured through the sacrifices of fallen service members. The ceremony provides an opportunity for the community to come together in unity and gratitude.

"Take a moment this Memorial Day to reflect, honor, and remember those who gave everything for our freedom," organizers stated in their invitation.

All community members are welcome to attend this meaningful tribute to America's heroes.
