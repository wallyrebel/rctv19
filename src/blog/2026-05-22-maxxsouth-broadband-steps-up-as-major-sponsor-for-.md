# MaxxSouth Broadband Supports Burnside Music Fest with Major Sponsorship

MaxxSouth Broadband has announced its sponsorship of the Burnside Music Fest, joining forces with organizers to support live music and cultural programming in historic downtown Ripley.

## Community Partnership

The broadband provider's contribution comes at a crucial time for the festival, which relies on community support to continue delivering quality entertainment and attracting visitors to the area. The sponsorship demonstrates MaxxSouth Broadband's dedication to fostering local arts and cultural events.

## Impact on Downtown Ripley

The Burnside Music Fest plays a vital role in bringing energy and economic activity to Ripley's historic downtown district. By supporting the event, MaxxSouth Broadband is helping to ensure that residents and visitors can continue to enjoy live music performances and community gatherings.

## Local Business Commitment

This sponsorship reflects MaxxSouth Broadband's broader commitment to the communities it serves. The company recognizes that vibrant cultural events strengthen neighborhoods and create lasting connections among residents.

Organizers expressed gratitude for the partnership, noting that corporate support from businesses like MaxxSouth Broadband is essential to keeping the festival thriving and accessible to the community.
