# 19Sevn7 Permanent Jewelry Brings Custom Craftsmanship to Burnside Music Fest

The 2026 Burnside Music Fest is expanding its vendor offerings with the addition of 19Sevn7 Permanent Jewelry, a custom jewelry maker specializing in bespoke permanent pieces.

## Festival Details

The event will take place on **Saturday, June 6, 2026**, in the heart of historic downtown Ripley, offering attendees a full day of entertainment and shopping.

## What to Expect

Visitors to the 19Sevn7 booth will have the opportunity to design and create custom-fitted permanent jewelry pieces on-site. Whether seeking a meaningful keepsake to commemorate the festival experience or looking to add a stylish new accessory to their collection, attendees can work directly with the vendor to craft something uniquely personal.

## A Complete Festival Experience

The permanent jewelry vendor joins a broader lineup of attractions at the Burnside Music Fest, which will feature live Hill Country blues performances, diverse food vendors, and local shopping opportunities throughout the day. The festival celebrates the charm and character of historic downtown Ripley, making it an ideal setting for discovering local artisans and craftspeople.

## Plan Your Visit

Mark your calendars for June 6, 2026, and be sure to stop by the 19Sevn7 booth to explore custom permanent jewelry options. For more information about the festival, follow along using #BurnsideMusicFest on social media.
