# Downtown Ripley Comes Alive Tonight With Live Music, Food, and Fireworks

Downtown Ripley is gearing up for a vibrant summer Friday evening, offering residents and visitors a perfect blend of local shopping, dining, and entertainment.

## Evening Entertainment Kicks Off at 7:00 PM

The festivities begin at 7:00 PM at The Cut-Off, where live music from Trevor Wilson will set the tone for a relaxed summer evening. Attendees are encouraged to bring lawn chairs and settle in with friends as they enjoy the laid-back atmosphere of downtown Ripley.

## Food and Refreshments Available

Local food vendors will be on hand to keep guests satisfied throughout the evening. Chula's Food Truck will be serving up delicious fare, while The Gathering Porch will offer refreshing beverages to combat the summer heat.

## Grand Finale: Fireworks at 9:30 PM

The evening will culminate with the City of Ripley's Fireworks Show at 9:30 PM, launching near Ripley High School. Spectators have two excellent viewing options: the official viewing area along the highway or the scenic vantage point downtown at The Cut-Off.

## A Community Celebration

City organizers are encouraging residents to make this a special evening by shopping local, supporting downtown businesses, and celebrating together as a community. Whether you're looking for a full evening of entertainment or planning to catch just the fireworks finale, downtown Ripley promises something for everyone this Friday night.
