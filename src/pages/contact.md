## News Tips

Have a story we should cover? We want to hear about it!

- **Breaking News**: Call our hotline at (662) 837-9088
- **Story Ideas**: Email rctv19@gmail.com with "Story Idea" in the subject line
- **Event Announcements**: Submit at least two weeks in advance

## Advertising

RCTV19 offers affordable advertising opportunities for local businesses:

- **On-Air Commercials** - Reach viewers during our broadcasts
- **Website Sponsorship** - Banner ads on rctv19.com
- **Event Sponsorship** - Associate your brand with community events

For advertising rates and information, email **rctv19@gmail.com** or call (662) 837-9088.

## Technical Support

Having trouble watching our stream?

- Check our [Watch Live](/watch-live/) page for troubleshooting tips

- Include your device type and browser for faster assistance
