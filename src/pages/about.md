## Our Mission

RCTV19 - Ripley Community Television is dedicated to serving the Ripley and Tippah County community by providing local news, events coverage, and community-focused programming that matters to our neighbors.

Since our founding in 1999, we have been committed to being the voice of our community, covering the stories that affect your daily life and celebrating the people and events that make our area special.

## What We Cover

Our programming includes:

- **Local News** - Breaking news, city council meetings, school board decisions, and community updates
- **Sports** - High school athletics, community leagues, and recreational sports
- **Community Events** - Parades, festivals, fundraisers, and local celebrations
- **Business Spotlight** - Features on local businesses and entrepreneurs
- **Weather** - Local forecasts and severe weather coverage
- **Public Affairs** - Interviews with community leaders and civic discussions

## Watch Us

You can watch RCTV19 in several ways:

- **Cable Channel 19** - Available to local cable subscribers
- **Live Stream** - Watch anytime on Roku, Amazon Fire or at [our streaming page](/watch-live/)
- **On Demand** - Catch up on stories you missed on our website
